About ReWear

ReWear is a community-driven platform that empowers people to give their unused clothing a new life. Our goal is to reduce textile waste and promote sustainability through mindful fashion choices.

Each year, millions of garments are discarded and end up in landfills. At ReWear, we believe that one person's unused outfit could be another's treasure. By enabling clothing swaps and a point-based redemption system, we help extend the lifecycle of wearable clothes.
